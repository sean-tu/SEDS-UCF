Fall 2017 Projects
See what SEDS members are doing this semester.

FSGC Hybrid Rocket Competition

Sponsored by Florida Space Grant Consortium
  • Objective: to build and launch a hybrid powered rocket.
  • There are two categories of competition: maximum altitude and closest to 2,000 feet.
  • Runs from August 2017 - March 2018
  • Team Lead application

IREC

Intercollegiate Rocketry Engineering Competition
  • High powered rocketry altitude competition to 10,000 or 30,000 feet.
  • With a payload size of 8.8 pounds, competing rockets are typically 4 to 8 inches in diameter and 8 to 20 ft. long.
  • Multistage rockets and all chemical propulsion types (solid, liquid, and hybrid) are allowed.
  • Runs from August 2017 - June 2018
  • Team Lead application

Design Build Launch

SEDS-UCF Competition
  • A friendly altitude competition designed to be a first taste of high-powered rocketry without needing a NAR L1 certification.
  • Workshops held throughout the Fall semester to take you through the basics of high-powered rocketry.
  • Runs August 2017 - May 2018
